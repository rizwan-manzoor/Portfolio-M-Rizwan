CFMOTO WORLD

A comprehensive vehicle management system for dealers, service personnel, and owners, streamlining maintenance tracking, warranty management, and service history through a user-friendly platform built with Django and React.

Technologies Used:

React JS | Django | PostGreSQL |AWS EC2 | S3

A vehicle management system designed for dealers, service personnel, and vehicle owners. Key features include vehicle maintenance tracking, warranty management, and user-specific tutorials and guides. The platform supports different user roles: Dealers manage inventory and sales, Service Personnel handle repairs and maintenance, and Vehicle Owners track the health and service history of their vehicles. Built on Django (backend) and React (frontend), this solution streamlines vehicle operations and enhances user experience through a comprehensive interface.
