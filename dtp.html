Dealership Toolkit Portal: Empowering Dealerships with Data-Driven Sales Optimization

The Dealership Toolkit Portal is a SaaS-based product developed to empower dealerships and franchises in the automobile and mobile sports industry. It addresses the challenges of customer identification, conversion, appointment management, and sales optimization through data-driven analytics. A thorough sales insights software!

Technologies Used:

React JS | React Native | Node JS | Express JS | PostGreSQL | AWS

After taking a comprehensive approach to developing a highly complex web-based application, Cosmic MarketVerse AI delivered significant improvements for investors and financial analysts.

  • Improved Sales Conversions
  • Efficient Appointment Management
  • Comprehensive Reporting
